* Theory
To determine the maximum alignment score, maximize $V(i, j)$, where $s$ is the
substitution matrix and $d$ is the gap penalty:
\begin{*align}
V(i,j) &= max
\begin{cases}
&V(i - 1, j - 1) + s(x_i, y_j), i = j\\
&V(i, j - 1) + d, i < j\\
&V(i - 1, j) + d, i > j\\
\end{cases}
\end{*align}
